About Us

Our Story

Where Every Moment Becomes Evalasting

Evalasting Events was born from a simple belief: the best celebrations don’t just look beautiful — they feel unforgettable. What began as a love for bringing people together has grown into a full-service event studio known for bold, glamorous design and flawless execution.

We specialize in milestone celebrations of every kind — luxe birthday parties and baby showers, prom send-offs, and elegant wedding receptions — wrapped in custom décor, lush balloon installations, and show-stopping rentals. From the first sketch to the final teardown, our team handles the details so your day feels effortless.

Based in Los Angeles County & Inland Empire and serving the surrounding areas, we’d be honored to design your next celebration.
