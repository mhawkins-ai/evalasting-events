Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We’re based in Los Angeles County & Inland Empire and serve the surrounding areas. Traveling for your event may be possible — just ask when you inquire.

How far in advance should I book?

The earlier the better, as popular dates book quickly. That said, we love a beautiful challenge and will do our best to accommodate shorter timelines when our calendar allows.

Can I book decorations or rentals without full event planning?

Absolutely. You can book full-service planning, or choose décor and rentals à la carte — whatever your celebration needs.

What happens after I submit an inquiry?

Your inquiry comes straight to our team. We’ll reach out to schedule a consultation, learn about your vision, and put together a custom proposal.
